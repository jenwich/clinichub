What is ClinicHub

ClinicHub is an E-commerce website designed to be the center of community between doctors and patients, the health information center and the health utility center. The Doctors can contribute to the clinic seen as a station and the patients can create the private virtual room (called session) that the doctor will enter the room by the system algorithm operating depend on something such as illness type, priority, available doctors and instruments, then the doctor will perform a health advisory.
